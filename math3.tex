    when $D=q^2+p^3>0$. These are given by Cardan's formual as
    \begin{equation}
        y_1=u+v,\quad y_2=-\frac{u+v}{2} + i/2\sqrt{3}(u-v),\\ \quad y_3=-\frac{u+v}{2} - i/2\sqrt{3}(u-v)
    \end{equation}
    where
    \begin{equation}
        u=\sqrt[3]{-q+\sqrt{q^2+p^3}},\qquad v=\sqrt[3]{-q-\sqrt{q^2+p^3}}
    \end{equation}

    \end{equation}
